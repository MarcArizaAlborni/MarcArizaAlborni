About Me

Hello, I am Marc Ariza Alborni and welcome to my personal page!

I have just completed my Degree in Videogame Design & Development at CITM-UPC (Barcelona).

My main focus and interest in Video Game Development is the Design department. I have been playing video games since quite a young age starting with the PS2 and Star Wars Battlefront. I always complete every game I find, this is because as a Designer I believe that understanding what and what does not work in a game and to know why they are designed in a certain way is what leads to a good design overall.

Having said this I also believe that a Designer should understand how the Coding part of the development work, this is why I have also focused quite a lot in code and programming languages, mainly C/C++.
